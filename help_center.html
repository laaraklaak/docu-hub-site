The Decoder

Government terminology can be confusing. Here is what they mean versus what we actually need from you.

Agency Language:

"Submit biographic documentation in 600 DPI non-glare format."

Docu-Hub Meaning:

Take a high-quality photo of your ID with your phone. Do it near a window (natural light) to avoid a flash-glare on the plastic.

Agency Language:

"Signed Bearer Authorization with JP seal required."

Docu-Hub Meaning:

Sign the digital pad on our form. If your specific task needs a JP, we will coordinate the physical stamping for you.

Registry FAQ

Why was my passport photo rejected before?
PICA is strict about shadows behind the head, glare on glasses, and hair covering the ears. Our intake officers pre-vet your photo. If it won't pass, weâ€™ll tell you before submission.
How secure is my data? (JDPA)
We comply with the Data Protection Act (2020). All digital ID scans are permanently purged 30 days after your document is handed over. We do not keep ghost copies.
Can I pay in cash? (POCA)
Per POCA AML standards, we minimize cash handling. All payments are via bank transfer or online deposit to create a clear digital audit trail for your protection and ours.
What if I lost my TRN slip?
We don't need the physical slip. If you are registered in the Jamaican system, we can retrieve your number using your legal name and birth details via our agency liaison.
