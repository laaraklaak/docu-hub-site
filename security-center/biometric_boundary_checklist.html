The Biometric Boundary

Understanding the division of labor for secure government applications.

What Docu-Hub Does ADMINISTRATIVE
  • Form Preparation: Completing complex government applications with zero errors.
  • Fee Payment: Handling all statutory fees online or at the cashier window.
  • The Queue: Standing in line for drop-offs and non-biometric submissions.
  • Logistics: Organizing JP certification and secure document transport.
What YOU Must Do IDENTITY
  • Fingerprints: Physically attend JCF appointments for biometric scanning.
  • Live Photos: Be present for official photos at PICA or Immigration.
  • Wet Signature: Sign the final application form to verify truthfulness.
  • Interviews: Attend any mandatory consulate or agency interviews.

Why this matters: This strict separation protects your identity. Any agent promising to handle fingerprints or live photos for you is violating security protocols. Our compliance ensures your documents are legally indisputable.
