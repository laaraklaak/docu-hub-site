Why We Delete Your Documents in 30 Days.

Explaining our "Dual-Clock" Retention Strategy under the JDPA.

In the digital age, most people think "good service" means keeping client files forever "just in case." At Docu-Hub, we believe security means deleting data when it's no longer needed.

If a bearer keeps a photo of your Passport on their phone for 2 years, what happens when they lose that phone? Your identity is compromised. To prevent this, we operate under a strict Dual-Clock Strategy.

Clock 1: The 30-Day Purge

Action: PERMANENT DELETION

Data: Birth Certificates, ID Scans, draft PICA/RGD forms.

"Wi wipe yuh sensitive paper dem clean after 30 days cause wi nuh want yuh business a sit down pon wi system forever."

Clock 2: The 7-Year Vault

Action: SECURE ARCHIVE

Data: Signed Contracts, Authorization Letters, TAJ Receipts.

"Wi lock up di contract an' di receipt dem fi seven year, cause di law (POCA) seh wi haffi prove seh wi move di money straight."

Why This Separation Matters

This "Dual-Clock" rule ensures that even if our daily working folders were ever targeted, your permanent identification data wouldn't be there to be found. We minimize the risk to you by minimizing the amount of data we hold.

That is the difference between a simple "Bearer" and a professional Data Controller.
