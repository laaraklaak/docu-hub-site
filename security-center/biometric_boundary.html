We Can Handle Your Paperwork, But Only You Can Handle Your Identity.

Understanding the "Biometric Boundary" in Jamaican government applications.

We specialize in making government logistics invisible. We handle the forms, the lines, and the payments. However, there is one line we cannot cross: The Biometric Boundary.

Modern security protocols at agencies like the Jamaica Constabulary Force (JCF) and PICA require "Proof of Life." This means for certain steps, your physical presence is mandatory by law to prevent identity fraud.

The Division of Labor

How we share the work to keep your application legally sound:

Remits Fees to TAJ/PICA "Wi pay di tax dem an' get yuh receipt." Provides Fingerprints "Yuh press yuh finger dem pon di scanner."

Why This Protects You

Be wary of any "agent" who claims they can get you a Police Record or Passport without you ever showing your face. This is often a sign of bypassing legal protocols, which can result in your documents being flagged or revoked later.

Our refusal to handle your biometrics is proof of our legitimacy. We prepare everything so your physical visit is fast—often under 15 minutes—but we ensure the final document is legally indisputable because you were there to validate it.
