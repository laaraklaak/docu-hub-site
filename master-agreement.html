I. Commercial Terms and Scope of Engagement (TOS)

This section governs the commercial relationship, fees, and strictly defines the services provided.

A. Acceptance of Terms: By engaging the Agent, the Client accepts all terms contained within this Master Contract, which governs all services provided by Docu-Hub Operations Services, whether initiated in person or digitally.

B. Service Scope: Services are strictly limited to clerical assistance, data entry, document formatting/auditing, payment remittance (Agency), and technical guidance (Digital Coaching).

C. Fees and Payment: The Client agrees to pay the fixed professional service fee (as defined in the invoice) plus the exact amount of any statutory payments (e.g., taxes) or third-party costs.

D. POCA/AML Protocols (Funds Handling):

  • The Client agrees to comply with the Agent's formal Customer Due Diligence (CDD) / KYC Protocol, including verifying identity and source of funds.
  • Payment Agency: The Client must transfer the total pre-calculated amount to the Agent's designated account prior to the execution of the service. Statutory funds shall be remitted to the relevant government entity on the Client's behalf.

A. Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL) Disclaimer: The Agent is not an attorney, legal advisor, or certified tax consultant. Services do not include legal opinions or interpretation of statutes.

B. D. Accuracy Warranty: The Client warrants that all information and documents provided are true, accurate, and authentic. The Agent reserves the right to terminate services immediately without refund if fraudulent documents are detected.

III. Data Processing Consent (JDPA Standards 1, 2, 5)

This section establishes lawfulness and retention limits for your data.

A. Data Controller Identity: Docu-Hub Operations Services (Proprietor: Laura Clarke) is the Data Controller responsible for processing.

B. Explicit Consent & Purpose: The Client grants explicit consent to the Agent to process their data solely for the timely completion of the specified statutory payment, renewal service, or application.

C. Data Processed: Includes Full Legal Name, Date of Birth, Place of Birth, Filiation Data, TRN, Residential Address, Employment History, and Assessment Numbers.

D. Data Retention Protocol (Storage Limitation): The Client acknowledges that the Agent shall securely delete or render anonymous all transactional processing data within thirty (30) days following successful completion.

EXCEPTION (POCA): In strict compliance with the Proceeds of Crime Act, all Customer Due Diligence (CDD/KYC) records must be securely retained in an encrypted format for a minimum of seven (7) years.

A. Security Measures: Client data is stored on encrypted devices and managed in accordance with the Agent's Internal Data Security Protocol.

B. Prohibited Data: The Agent will not store sensitive data (e.g., criminal records/health) unless required solely for temporary clerical processing where the client retains full custody of the final document.

C. Your Rights: The Client has the Right to Access, Rectification, and Erasure of their data outside of mandatory retention periods. Complaints may be lodged with the Office of the Information Commissioner (OIC) Jamaica.
