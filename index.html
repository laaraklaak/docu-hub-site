Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Data Collection Strategy

In accordance with the Data Protection Act of Jamaica, Docu-Hub Operations Services collects sensitive personal data (TRN, DOB, Government IDs) solely for the purpose of executing requested administrative logistics.

2. Dual-Clock Retention Framework

We operate a strict data lifecycle: Digital identifiers are purged within 30 days of service completion. Statutory financial records are retained for 7 years as required by Jamaican bookkeeping regulations.

3. Secure Disclosure

Data is shared exclusively with relevant government agencies (RGD, TAJ, NIS) and is never sold or utilized for marketing purposes.

Terms of Service

1. Administrative Nature

Docu-Hub is a private administrative logistics agency. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice or statutory interpretation.

2. Client Responsibility

Clients are responsible for providing accurate and truthful information. Any discrepancies caused by inaccurate client data are not the liability of Docu-Hub.

3. Refund Policy

Fees are for administrative labor and logistics. Once a courier is dispatched or an agency submission is made, service fees are non-refundable.

Legal Notice

POCA Compliance

All high-value tax logistics handled by Docu-Hub must comply with the Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA). We reserve the right to refuse service for transactions that do not meet KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements.

Agency Limitations

We do not guarantee the speed of government agencies. Our commitment is to the accuracy and promptness of the submission and retrieval, not the agency's internal processing time.
