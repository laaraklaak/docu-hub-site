Operational Protocols

Document Standards

All uploaded documents must be scanned at 300 DPI. Photos taken with mobile phones must be on a flat surface with good lighting and no glare. Blurred documents will trigger an automatic rejection.

Payment Protocols

Professional Fees are due upon engagement. Statutory Agency fees (e.g., PICA $6,500) are handled as "Pass-Through" costs and must be cleared before submission to the agency.

Turnaround Times

  • PICA Renewal: 7-10 Business Days
  • Police Record: 14-21 Business Days
  • Vital Records (RGD): 6-8 Weeks (Standard)
