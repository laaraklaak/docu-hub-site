Policy 01

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025
Data Protection Officer (DPO): Laura Clarke
Status: Registration Pending (Office of the Information Commissioner)

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Docu-Hub Operations Services, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your information. This policy describes how we collect, process, and protect your personal data in accordance with the Jamaica Data Protection Act (2020).

2. Our Status with the Regulator

In our pursuit of absolute transparency and compliance, Docu-Hub Operations Services has initiated the formal registration process as a Data Controller with the Office of the Information Commissioner (OIC). Our application for the 2025/2026 registration year is currently in "Pending" status as it undergoes verification.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Explicit Consent: When you authorize us to act as your agent for document retrieval or statutory filings.
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill the terms of our service agreement with your business or employer.
  • Legal Obligation: To satisfy statutory requirements under the Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA) and Jamaican revenue laws.

4. Categories of Data We Collect

To facilitate your onboarding and TAJ requirements, we process Identification Data (Full name, TRN, NIS number, date of birth, and copies of government-issued IDs), Administrative Data (Birth/Marriage certificates and contact details), and Business Data (Payroll statutorials and business registration certificates).

Note on Biometrics: We do not collect or store biometric data (fingerprints or official biometric photos). Clients must attend JCF or PICA offices in person for these requirements.

5. The "Dual-Clock" Retention Strategy

We believe in data minimization. Your records are managed under two strict timelines:

  • Category A (Transactional): Digital scans of vital records and draft forms used for clerical transfer are permanently purged within 30 days of the delivery of your final document.
  • Category B (Compliance): Signed service contracts, identity verification for business principals (KYC), and transaction receipts are archived for seven (7) years to satisfy POCA and audit requirements.

6. Security Measures

We utilize robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data:

  • Digital Security: All digital records are stored in encrypted cloud environments (Google Drive/Dropbox) protected by Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
  • Physical Security: Original certificates in our temporary custody are transported in lockable, tamper-evident travel bags and stored in lockable cabinets when not in transit.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the JDPA, you have the right to request access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, or erasure (subject to legal retention periods). Please contact our DPO, Laura Clarke, at admin@dochubservices.com. We commit to responding to all verified requests within thirty (30) days.
