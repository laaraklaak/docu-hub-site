Security & Compliance Insights

We operate under a "Bank-Grade" compliance framework to protect your identity.

Why Electronic Payment?

To comply with the Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA), we minimize cash handling. Bank transfers provide a verifiable digital audit trail.

The "Dual-Clock" Policy

We retain transient data (ID scans) for only **30 Days**. Financial records are archived for **7 Years** as required by law.

Biometric Protocols

Your passport photos are sensitive data. They are transferred directly to the government agency and never stored on personal staff devices.
