Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Entity: Docu-Hub Operations Services

Data Protection Officer (DPO): Laura Clarke

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Docu-Hub Operations Services, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your information. This policy describes how we collect, process, and protect your personal data in accordance with the Jamaica Data Protection Act (2020).

2. Our Status with the Regulator

Docu-Hub Operations Services has initiated the formal registration process as a Data Controller with the Office of the Information Commissioner (OIC). Our application for the 2025/2026 registration year is currently in "Pending" status as it undergoes verification.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Explicit Consent: When you authorize us to act as your agent for document retrieval or statutory filings.
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill the terms of our service agreement with your business or employer.
  • Legal Obligation: To satisfy statutory requirements under POCA and Jamaican revenue laws.

4. Categories of Data We Collect

Identification Data (Full name, TRN, NIS), Administrative Data (Certificates), and Business Data (Payroll statutorials). Note: We do not collect biometric data.

5. The "Dual-Clock" Retention Strategy

  • Category A (Transactional): Digital scans purged within 30 days of final handover.
  • Category B (Compliance): Contracts and receipts archived for 7 years to satisfy POCA.

6. Security Measures

Records are stored in encrypted cloud environments with MFA. Physical certificates are transported in lockable, tamper-evident travel bags and stored in lockable cabinets.
