How to Spot a Fake Agent

Many informal "hustlers" offer to process documents but lack legal standing. Before you hand over your money or ID, check for these red flags:

  • No Registered Business Name: If they cannot show you a BN Certificate from the Companies Office, they are ghost entities.
  • Parking Lot Meetings: Legitimate agencies have a documented process, not just a meetup spot.
  • No Contract: Never start a service without a written agreement outlining costs and timelines.

Why We Ask for ID (POCA)

You might ask, "Why do you need my ID just to drop off a document?"

The answer is the Proceeds of Crime Act (POCA). Under Jamaican Law, any agency handling transactions on behalf of another person must perform "Know Your Customer" (KYC) checks.

This ensures that:

  • Your identity is not being used by criminals to register fraudulent vehicles or businesses.
  • We have a legal audit trail to protect YOU if a government error occurs.

We are not being difficult; we are being compliant. A service that doesn't ask for your ID is likely cutting corners that could hurt you later.
