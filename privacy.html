Privacy & Data Policy

Version 1.0 (B2C/B2B)

1. Data Controller Identification

Docu-Hub Operations Services ("Docu-Hub") acts as a Data Controller under the Data Protection Act (2020) of Jamaica. We process biographic and statutory information for the sole purpose of government liaison logistics.

2. The "Dual-Clock" Retention Standard

In accordance with Standard 5 of the JDPA, we maintain data only for the period necessary to fulfill our liaison obligations:

  • Active/Transient Data (30 Days): Scans of Passports, National IDs, Birth Certificates, and Photos are permanently purged from our active systems 30 days after a case is closed.
  • Audit/Statutory Records (7 Years): Signed authorizations and financial receipts are archived for seven years to satisfy POCA (Proceeds of Crime Act) and TAJ requirements.

3. Security Measures

We employ "Bank-Grade" technical controls, including AES-256 encryption at rest and SSL/TLS encryption in transit. Internal access to client files is restricted via Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Data is shared strictly with the relevant Government Agencies (PICA, RGD, TAJ, JCF, NIS) as authorized by your signed Bearer's Letter. We do not sell or lease personal data to commercial third parties.
